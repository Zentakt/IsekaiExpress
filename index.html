About Isekai Express

Welcome, Driver! At Isekai Express (異世界エクスプレス), we're not just a VTC; we're a portal to an extraordinary trucking experience. Founded on passion for simulation and a love for captivating narratives, we blend professional logistics with the thrill of exploring new horizons in ETS2.

Our mission is to deliver satisfaction, build a vibrant community, and help you forge your legend on the digital highways. Join us, and let's transport your cargo to another world!

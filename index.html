VTC Guidelines

Welcome to Isekai Express! To ensure a fantastic experience for all our drivers, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Respect & Professionalism: Treat all members, staff, and other road users (in-game) with respect and maintain a professional attitude. No harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior will be tolerated.
  • Discord Activity: Our Discord server (https://discord.gg/uE96rbK95j) is our main hub. Please check for announcements and participate actively.
  • In-Game Representation: Wear the official VTC paint job and in-game tag (`[IKAI]` or `[ISEX]`) when representing Isekai Express, especially during official convoys or public events.
  • Job Logging: (If applicable, e.g., using TrucksBook) Ensure all jobs completed for the VTC are logged accurately and in a timely manner. Adhere to any specified speed limits for logged jobs if required by the VTC.
  • Convoy Etiquette:
    • Follow instructions from convoy leaders.
    • Maintain a safe distance from other trucks.
    • No reckless driving, ramming, or blocking.
    • Use appropriate voice/text channels for communication.
    • Be on time for convoy departures.
  • Activity Requirements: (Example - You can adjust this) We encourage regular participation. Members may be required to log a minimum number of jobs/miles per month or attend a certain number of convoys to maintain active status. Specifics will be detailed on Discord.
  • Game Modifications: Only use mods approved by VTC management during official events to prevent conflicts. ProMods usage may be required for certain routes/events.
  • Have Fun!: While we aim for professionalism, remember that we're here to enjoy the trucking simulation experience together!

Detailed rules and updates will always be available on our Discord server. Happy trucking!

Guidelines for Joining

To become part of the Express Isekai family and embark on your isekai adventure, please follow these guidelines:

  • Own a legitimate copy of Euro Truck Simulator 2 with current updates
  • Maintain respect for all community members regardless of skill level or background
  • Embrace the anime culture and isekai spirit that defines our community
  • Participate actively in company events and convoy operations
  • Follow proper trucking etiquette during multiplayer sessions
